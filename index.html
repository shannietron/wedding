Events

  • 23rd April Sangeet संगीत @ Tamara
      • An evening of fun and dance where Shru & Shan's family and friends set the dance floor on fire!
  • 24th April Reception @ Billava Association
      • Join in the festivities by congratulating our families for the upcoming nuptials!
  • 25th April Wedding @ Billava Association
  • Kasiyatra காசி யாத்திரை
  • A fun tradition where Shru's dad has to entice Shan to marry his lovely daughter instead of going to Kasi for a pilgrimage
  • Maalai Maatral மாலை மாற்றல்
  • Who will get the garland on the other first? In this ceremony, Shru & Shan are lifted by their uncles and compete to get the garland on the other first.
  • Oonjal ஊஞ்சல்
  • Shru & Shan will sit on a swing while being sernaded and blessed by the womenfolk who will offer milk and bananas
  • Mangalya Dhaaranam மாங்கல்ய தாரணம்
  • The moment is here. Shan will tie the thaali (sacred thread) around Shru's neck to a crescendo of music while being showered with rice thrown on us by our loving family and friends
